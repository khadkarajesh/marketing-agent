### **Prompt (English)**

You are given a description of a product.
Your task is to analyze this product and generate **10 examples of realistic problems a user might experience**, written **from the user’s perspective**, assuming the user is **not aware that this product could solve their problem**.

The problems should:

* Be clear, natural, and relatable.
* Reflect real-world pain points or frustrations.
* Implicitly connect to the capabilities of the product, without mentioning the product itself.
* Be written as short first-person statements (e.g., “I struggle with…” or “I can’t figure out how to…”).

```
User Problems:

2. ...
3. ...
```
